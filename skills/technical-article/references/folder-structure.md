# Folder Structure

Default article workspace:

```text
content/articles/YYYY_MM_DD_<slug>/
├── 00_context/
│   └── author_profile.json
├── 01_planning/
│   ├── classification.md
│   └── outline.md
├── 02_research/
│   ├── sources.json
│   └── research_notes.md
├── 03_drafts/
│   ├── draft_v1.<lang>.md
│   └── draft_v2.<lang>.md
├── 04_review/
│   └── checklist.md
├── 05_assets/
│   └── images/
├── code/
│   ├── README.md
│   ├── src/
│   └── tests/
├── <slug>.<primary-lang>.md
└── <slug>.<other-lang>.md
```

Use fewer folders for short private drafts. Use the full structure for public, researched, translated, or example-backed articles.
