# Product Brief Template

Use this structure for product briefs. Keep each section short unless the decision genuinely needs more detail.

## Status

Draft, In Review, Approved, or Superseded.

## Summary

One paragraph: user, problem, proposed outcome, and why now.

## Users and Jobs

- Primary users
- Secondary users
- Jobs to be done
- Current workaround or pain

## Goals

Measurable outcomes the product work must achieve.

## Non-Goals

Reasonable adjacent work deliberately excluded.

## MVP Scope

The smallest useful version. Include must-have workflows and explicit exclusions.

## User Flows

Numbered workflows from trigger to success or failure.

## Requirements

Specific, testable product requirements.

## Quality Bar

Usability, reliability, performance, accessibility, privacy, security, support, and operations expectations when relevant.

## Success Metrics

Metrics with event/source, target or direction, and review window.

## Risks and Assumptions

Separate known risks from assumptions that need validation.

## Open Questions

Questions that materially affect scope, priority, or feasibility.

## Next Step

Recommend `design-doc`, `spec`, `plan`, or direct implementation.
